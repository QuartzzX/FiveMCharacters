Police

FivePD Police Bill of Rights
These must be read to a suspect upon being arrested and being questioned. Improperly reading them or not reading them could mean that the suspect may have to be let go, charges dropped and even in real life cases, lawsuits against the NZ Police.

The caution is as follows:
You are being arrested/detained for (give reason) You have the right to remain silent, You do not have to make any statement, Anything you say will be recorded and given in evidence in court. You have the right to speak with a lawyer without delay and in private before deciding to answer any of my questions, Police have a list of lawyers you may speak to for free. Do you understand your rights?

If the suspect says no, then repeat it one more time, if the suspect does not answer then read it a maximum of 3 times as by then they clearly under it and are likely using their right to remain silent.

FENZ

FivePD Fire Information
Arriving On Scene

/hose - Get hose
/foam - With hose out, activates foam instead of water. Use this for chemical fires
/spreaders - Use these to get persons trapped out of their vehicle
/stabilisers setup - Sets up stabilisers to assist with getting persons out of their vehicles (/stabilisers remove - to get rid of them)
/jack setup - Sets up airbags to lift vehicle into air, controls displayed on screen
/watermonitor setup - Sets up a ground monitor for fires, E to turn on/off, Arrow Up/Down to change height of spray

Fire Types and what to use:
Normal Fire - Water, Extinguisher, Foam
Bonfire - Water, Extinguisher, Foam
Chemical - Extinguisher or Foam (Water will make it worse)
Electrical - Extinguisher or Foam (Water will make it worse)

St John

FivePD Ambulance Information
Going on Duty

Calls will generate from FivePD, 111 calls or other agencies requesting Ambulance assistance. Using the F11 Menu, teleport to one of the Ambulance Stations. To use the radio, press T to bring up your chat. Now type /radio and press enter. Right Arrow to change to Channel 2 (This will be set to Channel 1 by default. Ch1 is used by Police only). Press E to turn your radio on. Press T, up arrow and enter (so your chat says /radio again) to put the handheld radio away. You'll still be able to transmit. When talking over the radio, ensure you are not cutting anyone off, or talking over the top of them. The default keybinding to talk is Left Alt. Press F2 to open Vehicle Spawner, Scroll to St John, spawn the vehicles you want to use. Press F10, go to Rules and find Callsigns. This will help you with what callsigns you can use. Type in chat advising St John is active
